# Privacy Policy

**Simple Islamic Calendar**
Last updated: 4 July 2026

Simple Islamic Calendar ("the app", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your
privacy. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and
what is — and isn't — shared. In short: **we do not collect, store, or sell any
personal data, and we have no servers of our own.**

## Information We Access

### Location
When you grant location permission, the app reads your device's GPS coordinates
**solely to calculate accurate prayer times for your area**. Your coordinates
are converted to a city and country name on your device.

- Location is used only while you are using the app (foreground only).
- If you deny location permission, the app falls back to showing prayer times
  for Mecca, Saudi Arabia.
- We do **not** store your location, track your movement, or build any profile.

### Prayer Times Service (Third Party)
To calculate prayer times, the app sends your latitude and longitude to the
**AlAdhan API** (https://aladhan.com). This request is used only to return
prayer times and is subject to AlAdhan's own privacy practices. We do not
receive or retain any data from these requests.

### On-Device Settings
Your preferences (such as theme and calculation settings) are saved locally on
your device only. This data never leaves your phone and is removed when you
uninstall the app.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** create user accounts or require sign-in.
- We do **not** use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
- We do **not** collect names, emails, contacts, or device identifiers.
- We do **not** sell or share your data with anyone.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children, and
contains no ads or in-app purchases.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this
page with an updated "Last updated" date.
